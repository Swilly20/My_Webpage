About Steele:

Steele Wilson is currently a Computer Engineer at Florida State University. His hometown is Nokomis, FL Which is right next to the #1 beach in the USA, Siesta Key! Steele likes to spend time around his close friends along with exercising at the local gym. In his free time, Steele enjoys playing video games with his buddies or adventuring out into the day & night life in Tallahassee. With this, Steele finds it easy to balance between studying and activities outside the classroom by thouroughly planning out his week in advanced. At the end of his college career, Steele plans to find a company that can help him grow professionally and develop the work experiance he needs to become an engineer that will make a huge impact on the World.
